Painting Experience.

×

2017 My First Painting.

In 2017, I started painting for the first time in my life. I mainly tried to copy the characters in animation. It could be seen that the first painting was very bad. But I think the first attempt was very valuable and was kept.

The first unsatisfactory attempt didn't discourage me. I began to practice a lot and keep looking for information to improve myself. With my continuous practice, I draw better and better.

2018 Join the Academy of Fine Arts.

With my continuous learning, I gradually found myself lack of systematic learning. So in 2018, I joined the local Academy of Fine Arts in Shanghai to study traditional art. I began to acquire professional techniques such as sketch and color.

In the learning process, I learned essential theories. And my paintings are getting better and better. Among the three subjects of sketch, quick sketch and color, I was good at the course color. On the right picture is my color exercise.

×
×

2019 After the Academy of Fine Arts.

After finishing my study in the Academy of fine arts, I found that my painting level was greatly improved compared with that before. Gradually, I began to be dissatisfied with drawing with only a pencil. I began to learn to use more painting tools, such as color lead, marker, oil paint and so on.

On the right are some of my paintings. It can be seen that my level and have been improved to a certain extent. The picture is richer and the shape is more accurate. I gradually began to have confidence in painting. I think I can be a good painter.

2020 Start Digital Painting.

Although my hand drawing skills are constantly improving, I gradually feel the bottleneck and think it difficult to make progress. I feel that I can't further improve the fineness of my picture due to the constraints of tools, so I began to try digital painting.

In my first digital painting, I painted very slowly. A very simple painting takes me two weeks. I once wanted to give up. But I encourage myself to keep on. With my continuous practice, my painting speed and quality are constantly improving.

×
×

Nowadays.

Now I'm still doing digital painting with continuous improvement of painting skills. I can gradually create digital paintings to my satisfaction. At the same time, I not only paint characters, but also begin to try to paint scenes and combine 3D technology. I am very happy with this kind of exploration and learning.

In this process of continuous progress, I gradually began to enjoy painting and can concentrate on painting. This makes me feel very satisfied and happy. Now painting has become an important part of my life and everyday life.

It has been three years since we barely made geometric models at the beginning, and now we can easily make complex character models. I'm already learning how to make 3D animation. With continuous learning, I feel that I don't know more and more knowledge and skills. I hope I can keep learning to improve myself.
