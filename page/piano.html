My Experience.

×

First Period.

I have been learning piano since kindergarten. But I didn't like practicing piano very much at that time. During the whole kindergarten and primary school, I only practiced for about 15 minutes every day. And often need parents to urge me to practice.

So for a long time, my piano level was relatively poor. In many aspects, it is not much different from beginners. But I didn't want to improve because I didn't have much interest in piano at that time.

Middle School Period.

Now I like playing the piano very much. I practice for about two hours every day. In addition to practicing my favorite tracks, I will also practice some exercises to improve my basic skills.

In order to play my favorite tracks, I began to practice more. From 15 minutes of practice every day to 30 minutes, and then to an hour. My piano skills have been improved. I began to play my favorite tracks. In the process, I gradually fell in love with the piano.

×
×

Now.

Now I like playing the piano very much. I practice for about two hours every day. In addition to practicing my favorite tracks, I will also practice some exercises to improve my basic skills. Now I love the piano more than ever.

Many times I think, if I used to practice hard, can I play more advanced tracks now? But I will soon stop thinking about this aspect. I should start from now rather than regretting the past.

My practice.

I have been playing the piano for about fifteen years. But it is only in recent years that I began to feel the fun of playing the piano. Compared with me who didn't like playing the piano before. Now I like playing the piano very much. Now the piano has become a part of my life.
